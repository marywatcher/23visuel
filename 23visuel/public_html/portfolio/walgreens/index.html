Drugstore.com was a business owned by Walgreens.

Overview

For about 6 months I teamed up with Akane Sevigny (Art Director) and Marc Tweed (Copywriter) to design more than 60 online retail campaigns for Drugstore.com, a dot-com darling owned by Walgreens.
Our work helped email marketing team redefine current personas and design better targeted offers.
Weekly cross-channel campaigns captured social networks, ad networks and drugstore.com website.
